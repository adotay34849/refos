1. Coffee :-

Coffee is a **hot drink** made from the roasted and ground seeds of a tropical shrub ¹. It is one of the three most popular beverages in the world (alongside water and tea) and one of the most profitable international commodities ². Coffee is mainly popular due to its invigorating effect, which is produced by caffeine, an alkaloid present in coffee ². Two species of coffee plants, **Coffea arabica** and **C. canephora**, supply almost all of the world’s consumption ². Arabica is considered a milder and more flavourful and aromatic brew than Robusta, the main variety of C. canephora ². Would you like to know more about coffee?
